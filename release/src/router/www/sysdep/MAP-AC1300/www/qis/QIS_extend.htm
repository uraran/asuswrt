Add anther Hive
  • Please download the app in
  • Connect to your Wi-Fi, open the app and log in
  • Click + in the app and follow the procedure
  • Place the new Hive to the location you want. Plug in the new Hive, wait until the solid white light shown on top.
  • Press the "Pairing" button for 3 seconds on this Hive, then do the same to the new Hive. You will see the LED start blinking on both Hive.
  • When you see slow blink 4 times, it means your new Hive is on board.
